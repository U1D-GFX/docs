## Installation Steps

### 1. Navigate to your FiveM directory

```text
C:\Users\USER\AppData\Local\FiveM\FiveM.app\mods
```

### 2. Select the Addon You Want

Just select any of the addons you want from our [library](https://u1dgfx.com/library) and then drop them into your FiveM mods folder.

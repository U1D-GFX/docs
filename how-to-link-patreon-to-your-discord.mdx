# Patreon ↔ Discord Integration Guide

This guide explains how to connect **Patreon** and **Discord** so that supporters automatically receive roles in your Discord server when they subscribe to a Patreon tier.

---

## 1. Connect Your Discord Account to Patreon

1. Log in to your Patreon account.
2. Click your **Profile Icon** in the top-right corner.
3. Go to **Settings**.
4. Select the **Apps** section.
5. Find **Discord** and click **Connect**.
6. Authorize Patreon to access your Discord account.

Once this is completed, Patreon will be able to manage roles in your server.
